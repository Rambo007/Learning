RECORD-BREAKING NADAL WINS 9TH FRENCH OPEN

Rafael nadal beat Novak Djokovic 3-6, 7-5, 6-2, 6-4 to win his 9th French Open and retain his world No. 1 ranking.

  • Nadal became the first man to win at least one Slam title for 10 consecutive years
  • He has now won 5 French Opens in a row, breaking the record of 4 he shared with Paul Ayme and Bjourn Borg
  • In all, Nadal has won 66 0f 67 matches he's played at the French open, including 35 straight matches. He last lost a match there in 2009
  • With 14 Grand Slam titles, he is tied with Pete Sampras at No.2. Only Roger Federer(17) has won more.
